About IEPFA

The Investor Education and Protection Fund Authority (IEPFA) works under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs to protect investor interests and enable recovery of unclaimed assets.

Services

  • Claim Unclaimed Dividends & Shares
  • Investor Awareness Programs
  • Grievance Redressal

Investor Awareness

Workshops, Webinars, And Resources To Educate Investors About Financial Literacy And Claiming Rights.

FAQs

How To Claim? File IEPF-5 Online.
Documents Required? ID, Share Certificate, Etc.
